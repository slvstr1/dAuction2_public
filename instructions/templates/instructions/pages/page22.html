A Retailer may also sell Units. Selling removes the "X" of Bought Units. Thus, if the Retailer in the example in Figure 14.b sells 2 Units, the "X" marks of Units 6 and 7 disappear. The column now looks as in Figure 14.c.

Units 6 and 7 now no longer contribute to the total earnings. The total earnings of the Retailer are now 5 x {{ treatment.retail_price }} = {% widthratio treatment.retail_price 1 5 %} ECU. Verify this by inspecting Figure 14.c.


If the Retailer in Figure 14.b would now like to buy one more Unit, the Earnings are indicated by the Earn(ings) per Unit of the first unused Unit, which is in this example Unit 8 with Earn(ings) per Unit of {{ treatment.retail_price }} ECU.
