If your offer cannot be matched for all the Units, your offer is added to the Offer Overview. Thus, if the Offer Overview is as in Figure 20.a (Before), and you make a BUY offer of 3 Units for a Price of 2 ECU, it will be added to the BUY Units in the second row and the Offer Overview will look as in Figure 20.c (After).
