6.1 Offers Overview

In the box labeled “Offers Overview” you can see all the outstanding offers by you and your group members. Figure 20.a shows the “Offers Overview” box for a specific example with fictitious offers. The offers are ordered by Price from high to low. The column "Number of Unit" shows the number of Units that are on offer for that Price.

Thus, if you want to buy Units, you need to look at the SELL offers. {% if auction.itype == FULL_INSTR %} As you can see in Figure 20.a, the best (cheapest) SELL offer is in the last (third) row with a Price of 4 ECU. Presently, there is 1 Unit offered for this Price. The next-best SELL offer is in the second-last (second) row with a Price of 5 ECU. There are 3 Units offered for this Price. The worst SELL offer is in the first row with a Price of 7 ECU. There are 2 Units offered for this Price. {% endif %}

If you want to sell Units, you need to look at the BUY offers. {% if auction.itype == FULL_INSTR %} As you can see in Figure 20.a, the best (most expensive) BUY offer is in the first row with a Price of 3 ECU. Presently, there is 1 Unit asked for this Price. The next-best BUY offer is in the second row with a Price of 2 ECU. There is 1 Unit asked for this Price. The worst BUY offer is in the last (third) row with a Price of 1 ECU. There are 2 Units asked for this Price. {% endif %}
