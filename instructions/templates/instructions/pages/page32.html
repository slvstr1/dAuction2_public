20d After: a new BUY offer of 11 Units for a Price of 9 ECU has been made

If the Offer Overview is again as in Figure 20.a (Before), and you make a BUY offer of 11 Units for a Price of 9, you will buy all the outstanding SELL offers, and the Offer Overview will look as in Figure 20.d( After).

You thus get a total of 6 Units, 1 Unit for a Price of 4 ECU, 3 Units for a Price of 5 ECU and 2 Units for a Price of 7 ECU. As there are still 5 Units left in your offer, they will be added to the BUY offers.

The same logic applies when you make SELL offers.
