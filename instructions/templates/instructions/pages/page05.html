{{ Forward_market }}

{{ Forward_market }} starts with a fixed-time phase that lasts {{ treatment.time_for_forward_1 }} seconds. When the time has expired, the market is extended by a "CONDITIONAL TIME PERIOD" for maximally {{ treatment.time_for_forward_2 }} seconds. However, within {{ treatment.time_conditional }} seconds at least one new transaction must be made by some participants, otherwise the CONDITIONAL TIME PERIOD ends.

It will always be clearly indicated in the middle on the top of your screen how much time you have left. Figure 3 shows an example in {{ forward_market }} of the indication on the top of your screen for the CONDITIONAL TIME PERIOD.

When the CONDITIONAL TIME PERIOD in the {{ forward_market }} has ended, Participants have a short break of {{ treatment.time_for_forward_waiting }} seconds to review their choices.

{{ Spot_market }}

{{ Spot_market }} {% if not treatment.only_spot %}also {% endif %}starts with a fixed-time phase that lasts {{ treatment.time_for_forward_1 }} seconds. When the time has expired, the market is {% if not treatment.only_spot %}also {% endif %}extended by a "CONDITIONAL TIME PERIOD" for maximally {{ treatment.time_for_forward_2 }} seconds. {% if not treatment.only_spot %}Again{% else %}However{% endif %}, every {{ treatment.time_conditional }} seconds at least one new transaction must be made by some participants, otherwise the CONDITIONAL TIME PERIOD ends. It will always be clearly indicated in the middle on the top of your screen how much time you have left.

Figure 4 shows an example of this indication in {{ spot_market }} for the case that the participant is a Retailer who bought 24 units less than the UNITS DEMANDED.

When the CONDITIONAL TIME PERIOD in the {{ spot_market }} ends, and not all Retailers in the group have bought a number of units equal or larger than UNITS DEMANDED, the market trading is still further extended by a "PENALTY TIME PERIOD" for a maximum of {{ treatment.time_for_spot_3 }} seconds. We describe the rules of the PENALTY TIME PERIODs in detail in the next sections. Once all Retailers have bought a number of units equal or larger than UNITS DEMANDED, or if the time has expired, {{ spot_market }} finishes.
